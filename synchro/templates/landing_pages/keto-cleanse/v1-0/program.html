A thoughtfully designed 3-week program

week one

Goals

Preparing the body for ketosis and Modified IF.

How we’ll achieve this

Using your low-carb meal plan and supplementing with Ketobasis, you’ll stabilize blood sugar and improve your body’s efficiency in oxidizing fats.

week two

Goals

Adapting to ketosis, starting Modified IF.

How we’ll achieve this

In week two, you’ll be on a full ketogenic diet for the first time. You’ll also start ramping up your daily modified fasting periods. Daily Ketomanna, Ketobasis and Hydros will make this transition as smooth as possible.

week three

Goals

Thriving in ketosis and Modified IF!

How we’ll achieve this

This is where you really feel the magic that’s been happening internally. In week 3 you’ll be mostly adapted to ketosis, and we’ll further ramp up the length of your daily modified fasting periods to maximize cellular cleaning and fat oxidation. People generally feel fantastic in week 3.
