We’ve blended the two highest-quality proteins available – organic hemp and pea proteins – in an ideal ratio to deliver 15g of amino-complete, easily-digestible plant protein in each serving of Synchro Genesis.

You could eat perfectly and have a killer workout regimen, but if your hormones are functioning poorly, you’ll still feel drained. Maca root gently stimulates our hormone systems, helping to bring them into balance and full functioning.

Coconut Palm Nectar

We wanted the perfect natural sweetener for Genesis, and we’ve found it in coconut palm nectar. It’s minimally-processed, low-glycemic and free of the contaminants found in other sugars. One serving of Synchro Genesis contains only about 8 grams of sugar. In the context of this nutritionally-balanced blend, this small amount of sugar digests relatively slowly, giving Genesis a very low cumulative Glycemic Index.
