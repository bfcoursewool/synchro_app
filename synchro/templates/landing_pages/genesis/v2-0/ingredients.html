Organic Gelatinized Maca Root

A prized adaptogen for millenia, organic maca gently stimulates our hormone systems to bring them into balance. Genesis uses gelatinized maca root for easier digestion and absorption.

Organic Plant Proteins

We’ve blended the two highest-quality plant proteins – organic hemp and organic pea proteins – in the perfect ratio to deliver 15g of amino-complete, easily-digestible plant protein in each serving of Genesis. These proteins support metabolism and keep you sustained for hours.

Low-Glycemic Sweeteners

Genesis is sweetened with a combination of monk fruit, chicory root and organic unrefined cane sugar. This combination allowed us to achieve a perfect, balanced sweetness, but with only 4.5g of total sugars per serving. This means you can be sure the boost you’re getting is coming from the nutrients, NOT the sugar.
