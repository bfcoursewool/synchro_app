99% of nootropic products are BORING. Synchro Cognos is different.

Here’s the dirty secret of the cognitive-enhancement (aka “nootropic”) industry:
99% of products on the market today are just slight variations of the same conventional formula.

These products intend to put the brain into a hyper-stimulated, narrowly-focused, “process-oriented” state. In practical terms, if you’re wanting to hammer out some data-entry or clean out your email inbox, conventional nootropic products could work great for you. In our humble opinion this state is BORING and relatively low-value.

In developing Synchro Cognos, we were after something different; the genuinely inspired states of “flow” from which great ideas are born. And realistically, conventional nootropics are more likely to hinder you than be of use in realizing these high-value states.

We knew that it was possible to formulate a nootropic product that would reliably produce creative flow states, and do it in a way that was actually healthy for the brain. The task simply required someone with a deep knowledge of brain and body metabolism to be innovative and think creatively about the formula design process. (Thankfully, that someone was us)

Synchro Cognos creates the ideal conditions for creative flow states through the synergistic interaction of three distinct components:

Full-Spectrum CO2 Extraction

Our proprietary CO2 extraction of turmeric root delivers levels of turmeric alkaloids that are head and shoulders above other extracts. Unlike other turmeric golds, which deliver only one or a few of the root’s active alkaloids, our CO2 extraction boasts the full spectrum of turmeric alkaloids, just as you would find in raw turmeric root. As studies have shown, full-spectrum extracts of turmeric produce a number of health benefits that are lost with other extraction technologies, most notably the stimulation of the body’s antioxidant systems.

Liposomal Delivery

Turmeric is an incredibly powerful plant, but it does have a critical shortcoming: turmeric alkaloids are usually poorly absorbed by the digestive tract. We’ve overcome this short- coming by creating the world’s first true liposomal turmeric gold. Our liposomal formula utilizes a specific type of fat (called phospholipids) blended under highly specific conditions. This process effectively binds the turmeric alkaloids to the phospholipids, allowing for greatly improved absorption. Studies have shown up to a 6-fold increase in bioavailability when turmeric alkaloids are delivered via liposomes.

Black Pepper Fruit Extract

Extracted from organic black pepper fruit, piperine is an organic compound that dramatically increases absorption of turmeric alkaloids in the gut. Piperine works by inhibiting an enzyme in our digestive tract that would otherwise break down a large portion of the turmeric alkaloids before they can be absorbed. Studies have shown up to a 2000% increase in bioavailability when turmeric is paired with black pepper fruit extract.
